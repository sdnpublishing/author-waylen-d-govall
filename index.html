About Waylen D Govall

WD

Waylen D Govall

Waylen D Govall writes literary speculative fiction about the extreme edges of human experience — stories set in the final chapters of civilisation, where the choices that define us become impossible to avoid.

Thought-provoking, emotionally honest, and written with real literary ambition, Govall's work asks what it means to be human when everything else has been stripped away.

For readers who want their fiction to matter.
