About Waylen D Govall

WD

Waylen D Govall

Waylen D Govall writes science fiction at the darkest end of the spectrum — stories set in the final hours of civilisation, where the choices that define us become impossible to avoid.

Fragmented Skies: The Last Chronicles of Earth is his debut series, following the lives of survivors and scientists as an extinction-level event reshapes human destiny. Literary in ambition, unflinching in execution, and deeply human at its core.

For readers who want their science fiction to carry genuine emotional and philosophical weight.
